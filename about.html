What is BitMusic?

BitMusic is a web-based music streaming client, built

on top of Bittorrent Torque's javascipt interface.

The music is streamed as a Bittorrent file, and you upload

as you are listening.

How does it work?

I seed all the torrents to start with, but you can help

by downloading the actual torrent files and seeding them

from a client.

You can fork the project on GitHub.
